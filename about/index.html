About Me

Hello there! I’m not a programmer, and I’m not pursuing a degree in computer science. Blogging is simply one of my passions, and I have two websites where I express myself in different ways: Daily and Notes.

Daily blog

Daily ( stop server)is my personal blog where I share my thoughts, insights, and everyday experiences. It’s primarily written in English and serves as a platform for me to reflect on life, share my musings, and connect with others who appreciate a good read.

Notes blog

Notes is my technical blog, focusing on a variety of tech-related topics. Unlike my dynamic blog, Daily, Notes is a static blog where I document my technical notes, experiments, and discoveries. Whether it’s coding tutorials, tech reviews, or programming insights, you’ll find it here.

Why Blogging?

Blogging is my way of bridging the gap between my everyday life and my technical interests. While I may not be a professional programmer, I enjoy sharing my experiences and knowledge with others. Daily allows me to express myself in English, while Notes serves as my digital notebook for all things tech-related.

Feel free to explore both of my websites. If you have any questions or just want to connect, don’t hesitate to reach out. I’m always excited to engage with fellow bloggers and tech enthusiasts.

Thanks for visiting!
